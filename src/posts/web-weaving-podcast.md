I was a guest on [James](https://jamesg.blog)'s [Wonders of Web Weaving podcast](https://web-weaving.jamesg.blog/7) and it was such a lovely conversation. We talked about old teenage blogs, how I found the IndieWeb community, why I think now is the best time to be your full weird self online, and I accidentally held myself publicly accountable for a side project.

This was also my first podcast ever, so I was a little bit nervous at the start. Besides [my recent MC experience](https://ohhelloana.blog/css-day-mc/), I don't often do unscripted things, so there's always a small fear in the back of my head. What if I sound weird? Or what if I do some poor sentence construction because I can get quite excited talking? Or, the worst, what if I say a word out loud that so far I've only ever read?

Since then I've recorded another conversation for another podcast (which should go live soon), which admittedly I was far less nervous about and a lot more relaxed, and I have another one lined up. It can only get better from now on!
